Head First Objects-Oriented Analysis and Design

Product details

Language: English

Category: Computer Science / Object-Oriented Analysis and Design

Authors: Brett D. McLaughlin, Gary Pollice, David West

ISBN-10: 0596008678

ISBN-13: 978-0596008673

Description: "Head First Object-Oriented Analysis and Design" provides a refreshing and engaging approach to learning the principles of object-oriented analysis and design (OOAD). Authored by Brett D. McLaughlin, Gary Pollice, and David West, this book takes a hands-on, brain-friendly approach to demystify complex concepts and techniques in OOAD. Through interactive exercises, puzzles, stories, and visual aids, readers are guided through the process of understanding requirements, creating models, and designing software systems using object-oriented principles. From use cases and class diagrams to design patterns and refactoring, this book covers essential topics in OOAD with clarity and humor. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, "Head First Object-Oriented Analysis and Design" offers a fun and effective way to master the art of designing object-oriented software systems.
